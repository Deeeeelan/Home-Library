Home Library

The Home Library is an innovative and revolutionary new project that allows people from around the world to share their books and knowledge to those around them. Users can put up a list of what used books they would wish to allow others to borrow, and then others can search for those books and check them out. After the book is done being borrowed, the borrower returns the book, and it becomes available once more for everyone in the community to borrow.
